    About Us

    The Muslim Students Federation(msf) is an Indian student organisation affiliated to the Muslim Students Federation and holds the political vision of the Indian Union Muslim League.

    Muslim Student Federation (msf)

    Faslim T P is the president, Sinan Thottada is the general secretary, and Basith Kuruva is the treasurer of the Edakkad mekhala(panjayath)committee. There are seven (07) shakha committees affiliated to the Edakkad mekhala(panjayath)committee.
